Welcome To Ramoromisi Attorneys

Ramoromisi Attorneys Incorporated is a law firm located in Gauteng, specifically in Rosebank. It was founded in 2022 by its sole director, Thama Ramoromisi. The firm specializes in offering legal counsel and representation to a diverse clientele, including private companies, individuals, State-Owned entities, and Municipalities, on a wide range of legal issues. We take pride in being a South African black-owned law firm. Our services encompass a broad spectrum of legal expertise, which can be explored further in the detailed list of services outlined in our offerings.

Practice Areas

Welcome to our Practice Areas page, where we specialize in various legal domains to cater to your unique needs. At Ramoromisi Attorneys Incorporated, we are dedicated to providing expert legal services that ensure your interests are protected and your legal matters are handled with precision. Our diverse practice areas encompass:

Practice Areas

Conveyancing Practice

We excel in Conveyancing Practice, managing property transactions and essential document drafting. We also gather necessary property-related information, such as mortgage bonds and compliance certificates.

Notarial Practice

Our Notarial Practice excels in Notarial Law, offering document notarization and legal advice in this domain. We provide comprehensive solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Commercial Law

Specializing in Commercial Law, we offer legal advice, dispute representation, and expertise in corporate legislation and dispute resolution.

Labour Law

Discover our South African Labour Law services, including employment contract drafting, misconduct investigations, and CCMA representation. We also provide legal opinions and policy reviews for employment-related matters.

Litigation Law

Specializing in civil litigation, we offer legal advice, dispute representation, and assistance with agreements, including corporate legislation expertise and dispute resolution guidance.

Contract

Our expertise covers a wide range of contract services, including drafting, vetting, and negotiation. We excel in both initiating and defending contract-related disputes and have a strong capability in interpreting contract terms.
